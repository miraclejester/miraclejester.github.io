About the Project

Stink Bomb Revenge is a puzzle-survival prototype inspired by WariorWare and playable directly in the browser. A bully has stumbled into a stinky trap, and the player must figure out the bomb's logic and survive as long as possible before it triggers. The game layers puzzle-solving with a time-pressure survival challenge, requiring players to read and respond to the trap's mechanics under increasing urgency.

It was built by an 8-person team working on-site for three straight days at the Global Game Jam 2015, my first game jam experience. Built in Unity, compiled to HTML5.

Technical Highlights

  • Collaborative combination of mini-games coded by different developers integrated into one experience
  • Browser-playable via WebGL
  • Completed on-site over three consecutive days at Global Game Jam 2015

Features

  • Browser-playable with no download required
  • Timed survival challenge against the bomb's mechanisms
  • Prototype-level scope with focused design

What I Learned

This was the first game jam experience. Working on-site with a team for three straight days under a hard deadline is a different discipline from solo or remote development. Communication is king, integration problems surface faster, and shipping something coherent requires constant alignment. The experience established the fundamentals of jam-paced teamwork that I carried into every subsequent jam.
